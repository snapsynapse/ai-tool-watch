## Summary

Meta's Llama 4 family extends local/open model access into multimodal and very large-context self-hosted use.

## Deployment Modes

- self-hosted
- local-on-device
- local-api

## Common Runtimes

- LM Studio
- vLLM
- Ollama

## Constraints

- model size and context claims can exceed ordinary consumer hardware
- license terms differ from permissive Apache-style models
- multimodal support depends on runtime compatibility

## Related Capabilities

- see-images-and-screens
- write-and-explain
